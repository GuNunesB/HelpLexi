Website premise

Our project was developed based on the neurodiversity theme proposed by Etec Sebrae, bringing together the technical subjects from the systems development course and the subjects from the common base of high school, and on top of this theme we talk about dyslexia. With this theme we developed a website trying to reduce the difficulties that dyslexic people have when surfing the internet. Focusing on creating an inclusive and easily accessible website for neurodivergent people.
